The pixly app?

Technology used?

Sorry we did tried as much as possible not to re-invent wheel, picplx app is built on existing technologies as:
MongoDB, MySQL, Redis, Beanstalkd, Supervisor, Node.js, HTML5, CSS3, Playframework, Nginx and couple of hacks around. But for this open source version i have taken off some in house dev stuff, and what we think its necessary for public, we keep them open :). Hopefully during the second version release we will add more to it like how we are using Beanstalkd and Node.js, and other crazy tools :). Ola and I love building things and when we are not too busy we give ourselves challenges to hack things. We will release version 2.0 (which will be ported to Python/Tornado/Django) along side with its Android all open sourced by the April ending.

Who is behind the picplx app?

picplx app was developed by Ola White & Ebot Tabi of Geofeed Labs Team.

picplx's future?

Yes we will keep building more features into the app, making it more resourceful, fun and useful to the community. We plan an Android, BB apps when we get to version 2.0.

How do I use picplx?

Getting started is simple. Create an account, upload your pictures, add filters, add a location, start a story and quickly share them on Twitter, Foursquare and Facebook. From uploading your photos, meeting new people or making new friends, picplx makes it fun to share your photos, stories and daily events.
